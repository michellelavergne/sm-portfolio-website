— Michelle is a multi-disciplinary stage manager, software engineer and dog lover living in Miami, Fl.

Michelle is a graduate from the University of North Carolina School of the Arts with a BFA in Stage Management. Her career spans a vast array of theatrical mediums but she specializes in immersive theatre, large-scale opera, and straight plays- - particularly new works. Currently, Michelle is the company manager and resident production stage manager at Juggerknot Theatre Co., an immersive theatre company, and a resident production stage manager at Miami Classical Music Festival, an opera intensive summer program. When she's not in a theatre, she enjoys her time reading about true crime, playing video games, coding, raiding office supply stores and getting cuddles from her German Shepherd, Shadow.
